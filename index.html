What is Amipet?

At AmiPet, We Believe That Every Pet Deserves Care That's Not Only Loving, But Also Informed, Personalized, And Seamlessly Connected. We Understand That Pets Are Family — And Just Like Any Family Member, Their Health And Happiness Depend On The Right Balance Of Attention, Care, And Timely Guidance.

That's Why AmiPet Was Created — To Bridge The Gap Between Pet Parents And The Care Their Companions Truly Deserve. Whether It's Tracking Health Records, Finding A Nearby Vet In An Emergency, Discovering Pet-Friendly Places, Or Simply Understanding Your Pet's Breed Better — AmiPet Brings It All To Your Fingertips.
